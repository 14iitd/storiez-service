TOP 10 VEGETARIAN FOODS TO LOWER YOUR BLOOD SUGAR NATURALLY

Published By Moushumi Mahanta | 06 Jun, 2024

Nuts

Consumption of different tree nuts has been shown to lower fasting blood sugar levels in individuals with type 2 diabetes.
Credit : PEXELS

Pumpkin

Pumpkin contains polysaccharides, a type of carbohydrate studied for their ability to regulate blood sugar levels.
Credit : PEXELS

Broccoli

Sulforaphane, found in cruciferous vegetables like broccoli, exhibits blood sugar-reducing properties, potentially lowering the risk of type 2 diabetes.
Credit : PEXELS

Okra

Okra is abundant in compounds known to lower blood sugar, including polysaccharides and flavonoid antioxidants.
Credit : PEXELS

Flaxseed

Flaxseed, abundant in fiber and healthy fats, has the potential to lower blood sugar levels.
Credit : PEXELS

Chia Seeds

Chia seeds consumption may aid in blood sugar regulation, as studies suggest it could lower blood sugar levels and enhance insulin sensitivity.
Credit : PEXELS

Berries

Berries, rich in fiber, vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants, are an optimal selection for individuals seeking to manage blood sugar levels.
Credit : PEXELS

Kale

Kale contains compounds like fiber and flavonoid antioxidants that could contribute to lowering blood sugar levels.
Credit : PEXELS

Avocados

Avocados, abundant in healthy fats, fiber, vitamins, and minerals, may contribute to improved blood sugar regulation when incorporated into meals.
Credit : PEXELS

Citrus Fruits

Consuming whole citrus fruits may enhance insulin sensitivity and protect against diabetes.
Credit : PEXELS
