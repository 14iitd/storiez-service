5 scenic monsoon getaways in India to visit in June

The India Meteorological Department has predicted that the southwest monsoon will arrive on May 31, 2024. Here are five scenic destinations in the country to visit during monsoon season.

Video Credits: Pexels

Meghalaya

Photo Credits: Pexels

Meghalaya is home to stunning waterfalls and lush green forests that come to life during the monsoons. You can enjoy pleasant weather and breathtaking views here.

Photo Credits: Unsplash

Coorg

Photo Credits: Pexels

This serene landscape in Karnataka is dotted with cascading waterfalls and stunning tea plantations.

Photo Credits: HT File Photo

Kodaikanal

Photo Credits: Unsplash

Located in Tamil Nadu, Kodaikanal is surrounded by mystic forests, pristine rivers, and lakes that offer scenic beauty.

Photo Credits: Unsplash

Munnar

Photo Credits: HT File Photo

You can enjoy the lush green landscapes of this dreamy hill station in Kerala amidst its peaceful and calm nature.

Photo Credits: Unsplash

Lonavala

Photo Credits: Flickr

Lonavala is nestled in the lap of the Sahyadri mountains and surrounded by mystic hills and waterfalls. It is an ideal weekend getaway trip in Maharashtra to visit with your loved ones.
